Welcome to SUNY New Paltz

SUNY New Paltz is a vibrant, diverse, and innovative university located in the heart of the Hudson Valley. We offer a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and support services to help our students succeed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable education to students from all backgrounds. We are committed to fostering a community of learning, discovery, and engagement.
