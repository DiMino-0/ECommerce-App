Athletics at SUNY New Paltz

Discover the various athletic programs and teams at SUNY New Paltz. Our athletics department offers a wide range of sports and recreational activities for students of all skill levels.

Sports Teams

We have competitive teams in sports such as basketball, soccer, volleyball, and more. Our teams compete in the NCAA Division III and have a strong tradition of excellence.

Recreational Activities

In addition to our varsity sports teams, we offer a variety of recreational activities, including intramural sports, fitness classes, and outdoor adventures.
